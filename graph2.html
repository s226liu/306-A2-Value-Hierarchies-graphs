Value Hierarchy 2

Stakeholder 1: Women stressed about cycle uncertainty
Intrinsic Value: Well-being
  • Level 1 — Value
    Well-being
    • Level 2 — Objective
      Reduce anxiety around cycle uncertainty.
      • Requirement
        Pair every uncertainty message with a reassurance statement (e.g., “Cycle variation can be normal.”).
      • Requirement
        Avoid using alarming terms such as “abnormal” or “warning” in non-critical contexts.
      • Requirement
        Provide a short explanation panel on factors that affect cycle variability.
      • Requirement
        Display prediction ranges instead of fixed dates.
    • Level 2 — Objective
      Support informed body awareness.
      • Requirement
        Provide monthly pattern summary showing averages and variation.
      • Requirement
        Offer optional educational cards explaining cycle phases in neutral tone.
      • Requirement
        Allow users to log symptoms without mandatory interpretation.
      • Requirement
        Include trend visualization over multiple cycles.
    • Level 2 — Objective
      Prevent stress-inducing messaging.
      • Requirement
        Disable push notifications that imply urgency unless user-configured.
      • Requirement
        Avoid red warning colors for non-critical information.
      • Requirement
        Replace deterministic language (“You will start your period tomorrow”) with probabilistic phrasing.
      • Requirement
        Require content review to remove fear-based framing.
    • Level 2 — Objective
      Give users control over notifications and insights.
      • Requirement
        Allow users to toggle prediction notifications on or off.
      • Requirement
        Allow users to hide ovulation predictions.
      • Requirement
        Provide “Log-only mode” that disables predictive insights.
      • Requirement
        Allow users to customize frequency of reminders.
